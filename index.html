About us

Tegas Usaha Jaya Sdn Bhd (956919-H)

Tegas Usaha Jaya Sdn Bhd is a leading information technology (IT) company with an ardent objective to be involved in the IT market that is notable nationally and internationally. Tegas Usaha Jaya Sdn Bhd was founded in 2011 with a founder that has over 20 years experience in Information Technology & Telecommunications (ICT) and the headquarter of Tegas Usaha Jaya Sdn Bhd is located at Selangor, Malaysia. Tegas Usaha Jaya operates in a wide range of IT market to provide solution with a satisfying result as our company is a result-oriented company.
